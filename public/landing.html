StoryFlip is a crowdsourcing storytelling project that can help you actualize your creative potential.The idea is simple: someone creates the beginning of a story, the others make up and post their own continuations. Feel free to make alternatives for stories that exist, start entirely new threads and, of course, read the posted content.Possibilities are endless: create vast branching tales, search for ideas, write fanfics or discuss what's on your mind. The purpose of the project was to produce a tool for easy mind collaboration.

Log in and get storyflipping!
